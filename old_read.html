{1:31} Jetur, Naphish, and Kedemah. These are the sons of Ishmael.
{1:32} Now the sons of Keturah, Abraham’s concubine: she bare Zimran,

{1:33} And the sons of Midian; Ephah, and Epher, and Henoch, and Abida,
